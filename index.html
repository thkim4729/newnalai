Newnal?
Newnal consists of Newnal ai, which learns
based on user data, and Newnal OS,
an AI-dedicated operating system.
  • Personalized AI
    Unlike traditional AI models trained on public data, Newnal AI learns from the user’s personal data to generate responses and actions tailored to individual traits and preferences.
  • Hybrid OS
    Compatible with all software, including iOS and Android OS, allowing integration into nearly any device, such as smartphones, vehicles, and home appliances.
  • Data Tracking
    Provides complete transparency by displaying the personal data that served as a basis for the AI’s responses and actions.
  • Appless Environment
    Moving beyond the conventional app-based ecosystem, AI handles all tasks including content creation, shopping, payments, reservations, deliveries, and ride-hailing.
  • Web3 Telecom
    Ensures complete privacy protection while allowing AI to handle calls and relay necessary information to the recipient based on the user's data.
  • Blockchain Security
    Secures all personal data using Web3-based decentralized storage technology, with real-time blockchain verification to ensure data authenticity.
Why Newnal?

  • Founding Team
    Bezalel is a seasoned web3 and blockchain tech specialist of Newnal. Since the early days of blockchain technology, he has been dedicated to researching and developing core technologies that break away from the centralized Web 2.0 server model, enabling individuals to reclaim data sovereignty and building a decentralized, personalized web ecosystem—realizing the original vision of early internet technology.

    He designed and developed InfraBlockchain, the world’s first public multi-blockchain technology without cryptocurrency, and led the development and operation of COOV, a blockchain-based vaccine pass system used at a national level during COVID-19. Currently, his focus is on integrating blockchain with generative AI, working on technologies such as Web3 AI, Web3 Telecom, Web3 AI ID, and Web3 AI Agent.

    He studied Computer Science and Database Systems at Yonsei University and KAIST. In the early days of web technology, he worked as a Web Application Server (WAS) software developer and was also a first-generation developer of mobile platforms for iPhone and Android. His contributions include developing South Korea's leading mobile map app and internet portal mobile platforms.

    At the peak of the Web 2.0 mobile IT revolution, he met YT, the founder of Newnal, and joined as the company's first developer. For the past 11 years, he has been an integral part of Newnal. Upon joining, he adopted the name Bezalel (meaning "in the shadow of God"), inspired by the biblical figure Bezalel from Exodus—a highly regarded engineer of his time who built the Tabernacle, the central structure of society during the Exodus. Like his namesake, he sees it as his mission to develop IT technologies and services that bring value and utility to both society and individuals and remains fully committed to advancing Web3 and blockchain technology.
    Joe Park is a developer who has been part of the wave of technological innovation since the early days of the smartphone era. From the moment the iPhone first emerged, he has launched dozens of services across various mobile platforms, including iOS and Android, solving complex and challenging development tasks along the way.

    With a deep interest in hybrid mobile platform technologies to ensure consistent user experiences and development efficiency in fragmented environments, he has cultivated a versatile skill set, often taking on design responsibilities when needed.

    Early on, Joe also developed an interest in blockchain client technology, building a profound understanding of data security and encryption processing. This expertise has provided a crucial foundation for Newnal’s personalized AI technology, ensuring secure processing and protection of user data at the device level.

    He has witnessed firsthand the growth and limitations of the mobile industry over time. While the industry began with waves of innovation, he observed its gradual transformation into a standardized, factory-like ecosystem, making him deeply aware of the need for change.

    Joe’s beliefs and experiences play a key role in shaping Newnal’s new OS architecture and personalized AI user experience. Having long contemplated the essence of technology and user experience, his expertise is now proving invaluable in an era where individuals can have AI uniquely tailored to them.
    YT Kim is the creator of COOV, a blockchain-based vaccine pass adopted by 43 million people in South Korea during the COVID-19 pandemic, demonstrating the feasibility of large-scale Web3 services.

    Recognizing the shift from public AI to personalized AI, YT conceptualized Another-I, an innovative approach to AI personalization. Kim has systematically defined the key types of personal data required and the methodologies for integrating them, enabling AI to replicate an individual's unique thoughts, speech, and behavior—both rationally and emotionally.

    Kim studied theater directing at Dongguk University, one of South Korea’s top drama schools. His academic foundation in character building and role creation influenced the development of Another-I, bringing a humanistic perspective to AI that diverges from traditional engineering approaches. This unique methodology has played a crucial role in making AI behave more like a real person.

    Prior to founding Newnal, Kim built extensive experience in the entertainment and education industries, which now informs his strategic focus on developing content-driven business models for personal AI. He also serves as a board member at i-Scream Group (EdTech, Media, and Art), where he actively contributes to pioneering AI-driven educational models.
