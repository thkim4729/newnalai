What’s Newnal?
Newnal is an AI-dedicated operating system that enables individuals to create their own AI using their personal data and utilize it across any device.
  • Personalized AI
    Unlike traditional AI models trained on public data, Newnal AI learns from the user’s personal data to generate responses and actions tailored to individual traits and preferences.
  • Hybrid OS
    Compatible with all software, including iOS and Android OS, allowing integration
    into nearly any device, such as smartphones, vehicles, and home appliances.
  • Data Tracking
    Provides complete transparency by displaying the personal data that served as a basis for the AI’s responses and actions.
  • Appless Environment
    Moving beyond the conventional app-based ecosystem, AI handles all tasks including content creation, shopping, payments, reservations, deliveries, and ride-hailing.
  • Web3 Telecom
    Ensures complete privacy protection while allowing AI to handle calls and relay necessary information to the recipient based on the user's data.
  • Blockchain Security
    Secures all personal data using Web3-based decentralized storage technology, with real-time blockchain verification to ensure data authenticity.
Why Newnal?
  • AI That Supports Your Life
    Learns from personal data to provide a tailored AI that reflects individual traits and preferences.
  • Personal Data Ownership
    Protects user data and ensures ownership remains within the individual.
  • Environmental Sustainability
    Enables AI learning without relying on large data centers or excessive power consumption, reducing carbon emission.
  • Strong AI Ethics
    Enables blockchain-based verification of personal data authenticity, preventing deepfake AI and data manipulation while protecting data ownership and copyrights.

Who Create
Newnal?
  • Newnal Inc. was founded in 2013 in San Francisco by its founder, YT Kim. In 2014, seasoned blockchain specialist Bezalel Lim and OS architect Joe Park joined the company, collaborating ever since to bring their expertise to Newnal’s development.

    In 2017, they established and have been operating Blockchain Labs, a subsidiary of Newnal, in Seoul.
  • Founding Team
    Bezalel Lim is a seasoned web3 and blockchain specialist of Newnal. Since the early days of blockchain technology, he has been dedicated to researching and developing core technologies that break away from the traditional Web 2.0 centralized server model, enabling personal data sovereignty and building a decentralized web ecosystem—realizing the original vision of the internet as a personalized and distributed network.

    He designed and developed InfraBlockchain, the world’s first public multi-blockchain technology without cryptocurrency, and also developed and operated the COOV vaccine pass system. Currently, he is focusing on the research and development of Web3 AI Agents and Web3 Telecom technologies, which integrate blockchain and generative AI.

    He studied Computer Science and Database Systems at Yonsei University and KAIST. He started his career as an early WAS (Web Application Server) system software developer and was a first-generation mobile platform developer for iPhone and Android. He contributed to the development of Korea’s leading mobile map applications and internet portal mobile platforms.


    At a time when the Web 2.0 mobile IT revolution was reaching its peak, he met Newnal’s founder YT and joined as a core developer. He sees his life’s calling in developing IT technologies and services that bring value and utility to individuals and society. He is fully committed to Web3 & Blockchain technology research and development as part of Newnal.
    Joe Park has been at the forefront of technological innovation since the early smartphone era, launching numerous services across iOS and Android while tackling complex development challenges.

    With a deep interest in hybrid mobile platform technologies to ensure consistent user experiences and development efficiency in fragmented environments, he has cultivated a versatile skill set, often taking on design responsibilities when needed.

    Early on, Joe also developed an interest in blockchain client technology, building a profound understanding of data security and encryption processing. This expertise has provided a crucial foundation for Newnal’s personalized AI technology, ensuring secure processing and protection of user data at the device level.

    Having witnessed the mobile industry's transformation from rapid innovation to a standardized, factory-like ecosystem, Joe recognizes the urgent need for change.His insights and experience are instrumental in shaping Newnal’s new OS architecture and AI-driven user experience, making personalized AI a reality in today’s evolving tech landscape.
    YT Kim is the creator of COOV, a blockchain-based vaccine pass adopted by 43 million people in South Korea during the COVID-19 pandemic, demonstrating the feasibility of large-scale Web3 services.

    Recognizing the shift from public AI to personalized AI, YT conceptualized Another-I, an innovative approach to AI personalization. Kim has systematically defined the key types of personal data required and the methodologies for integrating them, enabling AI to replicate an individual's unique thoughts, speech, and behavior—both rationally and emotionally.

    Kim studied theater directing at Dongguk University, one of South Korea’s top drama schools. His academic foundation in character building and role creation influenced the development of Another-I, bringing a humanistic perspective to AI that diverges from traditional engineering approaches. This unique methodology has played a crucial role in making AI behave more like a real person.

    Prior to founding Newnal, Kim built extensive experience in the entertainment and education industries, which now informs his strategic focus on developing content-driven business models for personal AI. He also serves as a board member at i-Scream Group (Edu, Media, and Art), where he actively contributes to pioneering AI-driven educational models.
