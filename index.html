Newnal's
Mission
Generative AIs like ChatGPT cannot learn from your data because they cannot access data from all of your devices and services like Apple, Google, Facebook, Netflix, Amazon, and Chase Bank.
But imagine if you could gather all of your data to create your own ai - based on your personality and preferences, and capable of optimizing your decisions. It would enhance your life in practical and concrete ways.
Yet building your own ai comes with challenges.
To begin, how can personal data scattered across countless servers, each holding it in diverse forms and formats, be retrieved?
Once retrieved, by what criteria will the data be classified, integrated and labeled for optimal ai training?
And if all your aggregated data is stored on a company’s server or cloud, how can the risk of it being exposed in a single security breach be prevented?
Let us share our efforts since 2013 to overcome these challenges, leading to the development of Newnal ai.

What is
Newnal ai?
Data Learned by ai

Data Storage Space
My data is integrated on a blockchain web node that only I can control, not on a company’s server or cloud.
Verification of Data Sources

Why
Newnal ai?
1
Instead of an AI that gives everyone the same answers using web data, an ai should respond based on my traits, disposition, and circumstances.
2
Sensitive personal data needs to be stored in a secure space controlled solely by me, not on a company’s server or cloud.
3
Trust in ai-generated results requires verifying the sources of the training data. ‍

How is
Newnal ai
created?
01.
How can I
retrieve my data?
With the My Data Control Box, I can request, receive, and manage my data from companies worldwide through the Right to Data Portability*.
*Right to Data Portability
-
Right to request the transfer of data held by service companies
-
Protected in 44 countries, including the U.S. (CCPA), the EU (GDPR), and Japan (APPI)
02.
Where is my data stored?
My data is stored on a blockchain web node that only I can control, rather than on a company’s server or cloud.
03.
Don’t companies
provide data in
different forms and formats?
We identified data from 100,000 global companies and standardized it all.
04.
What are the
criteria for classifying
and labeling the data?
By retrieving and analyzing 210,000 questions that require personal data to answer, we designed the optimal data architecture to address them.
05.
How is the reliability of
‍ai-generated responses
ensured?
Using blockchain, anyone can verify the source and integrity of personal data used to generate responses.

OS / Device for ai

An app-less OS equipped with ai executes requests without requiring users to access individual apps. The ai-enabled device departs from the traditional form factor designed for mobile apps.
